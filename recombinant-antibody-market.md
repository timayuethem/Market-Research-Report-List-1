<p><strong>The Recombinant Antibody Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Chimeric Antibody</li><li>Humanized Antibody</li><li>Full Human Antibody</li><li>Single Chain Antibody</li><li>Bispecific Antibody</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The recombinant antibody market includes various types catering to different therapeutic needs. Chimeric antibodies combine mouse and human components, while humanized antibodies are primarily human with small animal-derived portions, enhancing compatibility. Full human antibodies are entirely engineered from human sources, minimizing immune response. Single chain antibodies consist of a single polypeptide chain, offering unique binding capabilities. Bispecific antibodies can simultaneously target two different antigens, making them valuable for complex disease treatments, particularly in cancer therapy and autoimmune diseases.</p></p>
