<p><strong>The Sleep Apnea Monitors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Channel Screening Devices</li><li>Actigraphy Systems</li><li>PSG Devices</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Sleep apnea monitors are essential tools for diagnosing and managing sleep disorders. Single channel screening devices offer a simplified approach, measuring basic parameters like airflow and oxygen levels, ideal for initial assessments. Actigraphy systems monitor sleep patterns and movements over time, providing insights into sleep quality and disruptions. PSG (Polysomnography) devices are comprehensive tools used in sleep labs, recording multiple physiological parameters during sleep, providing detailed diagnostic data for complex sleep apnea cases and other sleep disorders.</p></p>
