<p><p>The Copper CMP (Chemical Mechanical Polishing) slurry market comprises two primary types: Cu CMP slurry and Cu Barrier CMP slurry. Cu CMP slurry is designed for polishing copper interconnects in semiconductor manufacturing, ensuring smooth surfaces and minimal defects. On the other hand, Cu Barrier CMP slurry is specifically formulated for polishing barrier metals, such as tantalum or titanium, which are used to prevent copper diffusion. Both types are crucial for enhancing performance and reliability in advanced chip fabrication processes.</p></p>
