<p><p>The Electrostatic Discharge (ESD) floor market features two primary types: direct laying anti-static floors and anti-static access floors. Direct laying anti-static floors are permanently installed surfaces designed to dissipate static electricity effectively, ideal for environments like manufacturing and server rooms. In contrast, anti-static access floors consist of raised panels that allow easy access to wiring and utilities underneath while providing ESD protection. Both types ensure safety and efficiency in sensitive environments by preventing static-related damage to electronic components.</p></p>
