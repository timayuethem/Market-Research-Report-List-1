Wall-mount type machines are fixed installations, maximizing space in clinics while providing reliable access to anesthesia equipment. Table-top type machines are compact and designed for use on countertops in veterinary practices, offering a balance of portability and stability for routine surgeries and procedures. Each type caters to specific operational needs and environments.</p></p>
