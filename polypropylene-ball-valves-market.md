<p><p>The polypropylene ball valves market is categorized into two main types: two-way ball valves and three-way ball valves, along with other specialized variations. Two-way ball valves control fluid flow by opening or closing a single pathway, making them ideal for on-off operations. In contrast, three-way ball valves can divert flow between multiple paths, enabling more complex control within piping systems. Other market types may include varied designs or functionalities tailored for specific applications, enhancing versatility in fluid management.</p></p>
