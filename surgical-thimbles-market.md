<p><p>The surgical thimbles market is categorized primarily into two material types: nitrile and latex. Nitrile thimbles offer superior puncture resistance and are ideal for individuals with latex allergies, making them a preferred choice in various medical settings. In contrast, latex thimbles provide excellent tactile sensitivity and comfort, although they may pose allergy risks for some users. Both materials serve critical roles in ensuring gloves fit securely and enhance dexterity during surgical procedures, addressing diverse preferences and safety standards in healthcare.</p></p>
