<p><p>Waterborne wood coatings are eco-friendly finishes primarily used in the furniture, flooring, and cabinetry industries. These coatings utilize water as the primary solvent, reducing volatile organic compound (VOC) emissions compared to traditional solvent-based paints and varnishes. This makes them an attractive choice for environmentally conscious consumers and manufacturers seeking to comply with stringent environmental regulations.</p><p>The Waterborne Wood Coatings Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 6.7% during the forecast period.
