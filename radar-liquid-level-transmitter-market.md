<p><strong>The Radar Liquid Level Transmitter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>FMCW Radar</li><li>Guided Wave</li><li>CW Radar</li><li>Pulse Radar</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The radar liquid level transmitter market features several key technologies: </p><p>1. **FMCW Radar**: Uses frequency-modulated continuous waves to measure liquid levels with high accuracy and is effective in various conditions.</p><p>2. **Guided Wave Radar**: Employs a probe to guide microwave signals, ideal for challenging environments and providing precise measurements even with turbulent liquids. </p><p>3. **CW Radar**: Utilizes continuous wave signals for level measurement, suitable for stable environments but less versatile. </p><p>4. **Pulse Radar**: Sends short pulses and measures time of flight, offering robust performance across applications, including extreme temperatures and pressures.</p></p>
