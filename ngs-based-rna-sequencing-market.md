<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The NGS-based RNA-sequencing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Sequencing by Synthesis</li><li>Ion Semiconductor Sequencing</li><li>Single-molecule Real-time Sequencing</li><li>Nanopore Sequencing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The NGS-based RNA sequencing market includes several key types: </p><p>1. **Sequencing by Synthesis (SBS)** uses fluorescently labeled nucleotides to decode RNA sequences, offering high throughput and accuracy. </p><p>2. **Ion Semiconductor Sequencing** detects changes in pH to sequence RNA, enabling faster results at lower costs. </p><p>3. **Single-molecule Real-time Sequencing (SMRT)** allows direct observation of RNA molecules during sequencing, providing long reads and real-time data. </p><p>4. **Nanopore Sequencing** passes RNA through a nanopore, measuring ionic current changes to read sequences, allowing for portable and quick sequencing.</p></p>
