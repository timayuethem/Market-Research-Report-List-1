<p><ul><li>Sintered Rare Earth Magnet</li><li>Bonded Rare Earth Magnet</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The HDD market utilizes two primary types of rare earth magnets: sintered and bonded. Sintered rare earth magnets are produced through a high-temperature process, resulting in dense, strong magnets ideal for high-performance applications. They offer superior magnetic strength but can be brittle. In contrast, bonded rare earth magnets are made by combining magnetic powders with a polymer binder, allowing for more flexible shapes and sizes. These magnets provide good performance in less demanding applications, benefiting from easier manufacturing processes.</p></p>
