<p><p>The Printed Circuit Board (PCB) adhesives market is segmented into several types based on their chemical compositions. Cyanoacrylate-based adhesives provide rapid bonding and high strength, ideal for electronic components. Epoxy-based adhesives offer excellent thermal stability and durability. Silicone-based adhesives are known for their flexibility and resistance to extreme temperatures. Lastly, polyurethane-based adhesives deliver good adhesion properties and are often used for encapsulation and protection.</p></p>
