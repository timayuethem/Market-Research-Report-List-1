<p><p>The Dry Copra Coconut Oil market features two primary types: virgin and deep processing. Virgin coconut oil is extracted from fresh coconut meat through minimal processing, preserving its natural flavor, nutrients, and aroma, making it ideal for health-conscious consumers and culinary applications. In contrast, deep processing coconut oil is obtained from dried copra and involves refining, bleaching, and deodorizing, resulting in a product with a longer shelf life and a more neutral taste, suitable for industrial and commercial use.</p></p>
