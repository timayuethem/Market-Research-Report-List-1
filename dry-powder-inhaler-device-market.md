<p><p>The dry powder inhaler (DPI) device market comprises two main types: single-dose and multi-dose inhalers. Single-dose inhalers are designed to deliver one dose of medication per use, ensuring precise dosages and minimizing wastage, ideal for acute conditions. In contrast, multi-dose inhalers allow multiple doses to be stored and delivered from a single device, offering convenience and ease of use for chronic conditions. Both types enhance drug delivery efficiency and improve patient adherence to treatment regimens.</p></p>
