<p><ul><li>Injection Type</li><li>Oral Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The MRI contrast media market is categorized mainly into injection type and oral type. Injection type contrast media are administered intravenously and enhance the visibility of vascular structures and abnormalities within tissues during scans. Conversely, oral type contrast agents are ingested to improve imaging of the gastrointestinal tract. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing MRI diagnostics, with injectable agents often preferred for their rapid action and effectiveness, while oral agents are typically used for detailed gastrointestinal evaluations.</p></p>
