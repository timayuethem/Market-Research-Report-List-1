<p><p>The thin-film solar modules market comprises three main types: CdTe, CIS/CIGS, and a-Si. CdTe (Cadmium Telluride) modules are known for their efficient energy conversion and low production costs, making them widely used in utility-scale applications. CIS/CIGS (Copper Indium Selenide/Copper Indium Gallium Selenide) modules offer high efficiency and flexibility for integration into various surfaces. a-Si (Amorphous Silicon) modules, known for their lightweight and cost-effectiveness, are suited for smaller installations and less conventional applications, such as building-integrated photovoltaics.</p></p>
