<p><p>The Flight Data Monitoring System Market is divided into two primary categories: On-Board and On-Ground systems. On-Board systems are integrated within the aircraft to continuously capture and analyze flight data in real-time, enhancing safety and operational efficiency. In contrast, On-Ground systems are utilized at maintenance facilities and airports to process, store, and analyze this data post-flight. Together, these systems facilitate proactive safety management and regulatory compliance by providing crucial insights into flight operations.</p></p>
