<p><p>The Cold Spray Coating System market is divided into High Pressure Cold Spraying (HPCS) and Low-Pressure Cold Spraying (LPCS). HPCS utilizes high gas pressure to accelerate particles, achieving strong bonding and dense coatings, making it suitable for high-performance applications. In contrast, LPCS uses lower pressures, offering advantages in terms of equipment simplicity and cost-effectiveness, suitable for less demanding applications. Both methods are employed in various industries, including aerospace, automotive, and biomedical, to enhance surface properties and repair structures.</p></p>
