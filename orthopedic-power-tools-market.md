<p><p>The orthopedic power tools market consists of three main types: electric-powered, battery-operated, and pneumatic-powered tools. Electric-powered tools are highly efficient and provide consistent performance, making them ideal for surgeries requiring precision. Battery-operated tools offer enhanced mobility and convenience, allowing for freedom of movement without tethering to an outlet, which is beneficial in various surgical settings. Pneumatic-powered tools rely on compressed air to operate, providing powerful and lightweight options, particularly favored in high-demand surgical environments for their durability and efficiency.</p></p>
