<p><p>The pneumatic vises market is categorized into two main types: horizontal vises and vertical vises. Horizontal vises are designed for clamping workpieces parallel to the ground, making them ideal for milling and machining operations requiring stability during horizontal movement. In contrast, vertical vises hold workpieces upright, facilitating tasks such as drilling and precision cutting. These vises enhance efficiency and precision in manufacturing processes, catering to various industries by improving productivity and ensuring secure workpiece handling during operations.</p></p>
