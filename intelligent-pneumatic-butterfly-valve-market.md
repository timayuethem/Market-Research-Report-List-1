<p><p>The Intelligent Pneumatic Butterfly Valve market is categorized into three main types: Welding Type, Thread Type, and Flange Type. The Welding Type features valves that are welded directly to pipelines, ensuring a strong, leak-proof connection, ideal for high-pressure environments. Thread Type valves employ threaded connections, allowing for easy installation and maintenance, making them suitable for various applications. Flange Type valves utilize flanges for bolting to piping systems, offering versatility for larger pipes and facilitating rapid assembly and disassembly. Each type caters to specific operational needs and installation methods.</p></p>
