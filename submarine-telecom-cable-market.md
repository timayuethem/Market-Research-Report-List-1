<p><p>The submarine telecom cable market is primarily classified into two types based on their armoring: single layer armor and double armor layer. Single layer armor cables consist of a single protective layer, which offers basic protection against environmental factors and lightweight conditions. In contrast, double armor layer cables feature two layers of protective armor, providing enhanced durability and resistance against harsh marine environments and potential hazards. The choice between these types depends on environmental risks, installation depth, and required signal reliability.</p></p>
