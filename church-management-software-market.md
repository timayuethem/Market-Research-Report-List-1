<p><p>Church management software (CMS) can be categorized into two main types: cloud-based and on-premise solutions. Cloud-based CMS is hosted on remote servers, allowing for easy access from anywhere with internet connectivity, facilitating real-time updates and collaborative features. On-premise software, on the other hand, is installed locally on the church's hardware, offering greater control over data security and customization but often requiring more maintenance and upfront investment. Both types serve to streamline church administration and enhance community engagement.</p></p>
