<p><p>The semiconductor market utilizes various photomasks for lithography processes, primarily categorized into EUV, UV, and DUV masks. EUV (Extreme Ultraviolet) masks are essential for advanced nodes, enabling smaller feature sizes with higher precision. UV masks operate in the ultraviolet spectrum for less advanced technologies, while DUV (Deep Ultraviolet) masks are widely used for high-resolution patterning, striking a balance between capability and cost. Together, these masks play a crucial role in semiconductor manufacturing, impacting performance and innovation.</p></p>
