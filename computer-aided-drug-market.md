<p><strong>The Computer-Aided Drug Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Structure Based Drug</li><li>Ligand Based Drug</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The computer-aided drug market is categorized into two primary types: structure-based drug design and ligand-based drug design. Structure-based drug design leverages the 3D structure of biological targets to develop potential therapeutics, enhancing precision in targeting specific proteins. In contrast, ligand-based drug design focuses on the interactions between known ligands and their targets, utilizing existing compound data to identify similar molecules with desired biological effects. Both approaches streamline the drug discovery process, improving efficiency and reducing time to market.</p></p>
